About us

Zitto is an end to end e-commerce shipping solution powered by Click and Ship Limited. Launched in 2021, this solution facilitates a fast, worry free, secure and cost-effective shipping for individuals and businesses in the evolving world of e-commerce.

We are continuously adapting to the changing times to offer the best network solutions to deliver your goods and our staff are dedicated to offer an absolute service excellence. Zitto provides a seamless solution when shopping from the USA, UAE, EU and UK and thereafter forwarding your package to Kenya.

The service is a one stop shop from the time you click to the time we ship and your package arrives safely in your hands. End to end seamless service.

Click and Ship Limited is registered with the Companies Registry in the republic of Kenya, incorporated under the Companies Act, 2015 as a PRIVATE LIMITED COMPANY. Zitto is a trademark registered to Click and Ship Limited at the Kenya Industrial Property Institute.

How it works

Zitto is the super easy way to ship all your favourite online purchases from the USA, UAE, EU and UK. All you have to do is follow these 5 steps:
  1. SIGN-UP FOR FREE

    2. Get your very own virtual address in an instant!

  2. CREATE A SHIP REQUEST

    3. Enter the basic details of what you would like us to help you ship in. Name it, provide a short description and add a website link for the item. Our staff will review the request and get back to you with a quote and estimated shipment time.

  3. REVIEW AND APPROVE YOUR PAYMENT

    4. Once you review and approve the estimate, make the required payment and the rest of the process will be automated until the package arrives to your location.

  4. TRACK YOUR PACKAGE

    5. Sit back and relax, watch your package step by step. No more need to call or text. Enjoy free tracking of your package as it makes its way to your doorstep, all details available in your portal.

  5. SHIPPING CHARGES AND DELIVERY SCHEDULING

    6. Once the item has arrived you will be notified of the shipping charges and relevant taxes incurred for your review and approval. You can then schedule delivery of your item.

Prohibited Items

Due to customs and airline regulations, there are some items which are prohibited, and some which are restricted. Prohibited items cannot be shipped under any circumstance. Restricted items can be shipped, but additional approvals can sometimes take time, and add a day or more on to your delivery date. Make sure to check the list of PROHIBITED and RESTRICTED items before you shop.
